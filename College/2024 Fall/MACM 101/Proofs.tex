\section{Prove that if $3n+2$ is odd, then $n$ is odd}

Assume $n$ is even.

$n=2k, k \in \mathbb{Z}$

\begin{align*}
  3n+2 &= 3(2k)+2 \\
       &= 6k + 2 \\
       &= 2(3k + 1)
\end{align*}

$3n+2 = 2j$ 

$j = 3k+1$

Which shows that $j=3k+1$ is a multiple of $2$. (Even #)
